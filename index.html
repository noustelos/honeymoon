A Perfect Location by the Beach

Honeymoon Beach Studios is located in the peaceful area of Perivolos on the southeast coast of Santorini, just a short walk from the famous black sand beach.

Perivolos Beach is known for its crystal clear water, relaxed atmosphere, beach bars, and restaurants. Guests can reach the beach in just a few minutes on foot while still enjoying a quiet and comfortable stay.
